## **Objective**

As part of the Plan/Learn phase of your capstone project, demonstrate your
ability to plan and research by either gaining deep understanding of a specific
game technology or creating a game design document. This assignment builds
foundation skills for your subsequent prototype and final game deliverables
while creating portfolio-worthy content.

## **Method**

Decide early ie **Week 1** which path suits you. You have three options:

**Option 1:** Design a game and produce a Game Design Document (GDD), then
present your design so that others can join your team.  **Option 2:** Implement
an advanced technology in a game engine, then present it.  **Option 3:** Do
both — but you will only be graded on the technical development (Option 2).

All paths share the same milestones:
- **Present in class:**
  - **Design presentations (Option 1):** Wednesday, 22nd July 2026, starting at
    **14:30**.
  - **Tech presentations (Option 2):** Wednesday, 22nd July 2026, and may
    overflow into Thursday, 23rd July if needed.
- **Submit on Friday, 24th July 2026.**

If you take Option 3, you can present you game for others to decide if they
want to join your team, but the grade for this assignment comes solely from
your technical implementation.
